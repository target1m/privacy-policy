Privacy Policy for Kids Taboo Game

IMPORTANT NOTICE FOR PARENTS AND GUARDIANS: Kids Taboo Game is designed for children. We are committed to protecting children's privacy and complying with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Kids Taboo Game ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our iOS application. We are committed to creating a safe, fun, and educational environment for children while respecting their privacy.

2. Information We Collect

We do NOT collect any personal information from children.

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Location data or GPS coordinates
  • Photos, videos, or audio recordings
  • Social media information
  • Any other personally identifiable information

2.2 Information We May Collect:

  • Game Statistics: Anonymous gameplay data such as scores, levels completed, and game progress (stored locally on your device only)
  • Technical Information: Device type, iOS version, and app version for technical support and bug fixes (non-personally identifiable)
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data to improve app stability

3. How We Use Information

Any non-personal information we collect is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the game functionality
  • Improve user experience and game performance
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Ensure the app runs smoothly on different devices

4. COPPA Compliance

The Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) protects the privacy of children under 13 years of age. We are fully committed to COPPA compliance:

  • We do NOT collect, use, or disclose personal information from children
  • We do NOT enable children to make their personal information publicly available
  • We do NOT require children to provide more information than necessary to play the game
  • Parental consent is NOT required because we do not collect any personal information

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All game data is stored locally on the user's device
  • We do NOT transmit any personal data to external servers
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect any technical data collected
  • No user accounts or registration are required

6. Third-Party Services

Kids Taboo Game does NOT use any third-party services that collect personal information, including:

  • NO advertisements
  • NO analytics services that track personal information
  • NO social media integration
  • NO in-app purchases that collect payment information from children

7. Parental Rights

Parents and guardians have the following rights:

  • Review any information collected from their child (though we collect no personal information)
  • Request deletion of any data (all data is stored locally and can be deleted by uninstalling the app)
  • Refuse to allow further collection of information from their child
  • Contact us with any questions or concerns about our privacy practices

8. Data Retention and Deletion

  • All game data is stored locally on your device
  • You can delete all data by simply uninstalling the app
  • No data is retained on our servers because we don't collect personal information

9. International Users and GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Union, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • We process minimal data and only what is necessary for the app to function
  • All data processing is done locally on the device
  • We do not transfer data outside the user's device
  • Users have the right to access, correct, and delete their data at any time

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this policy
  • Providing notice through the App Store listing
  • Displaying a notice within the app (if significant changes occur)

We encourage parents to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect children's information.

11. Your Consent

By downloading and using Kids Taboo Game, you consent to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this policy, please do not use the app.

12. Links to Other Sites

Our app does NOT contain any links to external websites or third-party services. The game is completely self-contained to ensure children's safety.

14. California Privacy Rights

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). However, since we do not collect any personal information from users, these rights are not applicable to our app.

15. Commitment to Children's Privacy

We are dedicated to protecting children's privacy and creating a safe, enjoyable gaming experience. We understand the importance of maintaining trust with parents and guardians. Our app is designed with the following principles:

  • Safety First: No chat features, no social interaction with strangers
  • Age-Appropriate Content: All game content is suitable for children
  • No Pressure: No time-limited offers or pressure to make purchases
  • Educational Value: Focus on learning and fun
  • Transparency: Clear and honest communication with parents
